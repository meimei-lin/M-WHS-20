M-WHS: Myocardium Whole Heart Segmentation

Overview: Accurate computing, modeling, and analysis of whole heart structure are important for developing clinical applications. However, whole heart myocardium (cardiac muscleï¼Œheart muscle) image segmentation remains challenging and still relies heavily on manual work, which is time-consuming and prone to errors. The dataset provides 20 computed tomography (CT) cardiac muscle images acquired in a real clinical environment and is intended for the annotation of whole heart structures. This dataset supports the development and validation of whole heart myocardium segmentation algorithms.

Protocol: For pixel-accurate ground truth, the annotation of whole heart structures is performed by perfusionists and postgraduates. Their in-depth knowledge of cardiac anatomy ensures precise labeling, contributing to the accuracy and reliability of the dataset for segmentation research.
